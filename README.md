# 💰 Bank Statement Analyzer

**Turn a whole year of bank statements into one clear Excel overview — with AI. In minutes.**

Most coaches and entrepreneurs avoid their numbers — not from laziness, but because raw bank statements are chaos. This free tool turns that chaos into clarity: one clean, structured workbook that finally shows you the truth of your business year.

No software to buy. No subscription. It runs with the AI you already use.

---

## ✨ Why you'll love this

- 🪞 **Finally — an overview of ALL your money.** Everything incoming and everything outgoing, in one place.
- 🧾 **Not just totals.** Your **creditors** (what you paid suppliers) *and* your **debtors** (your real revenue), your **salary/draws**, even your **investments** — each on its own clear tab.
- 📊 **Your most important numbers and summaries at a glance** — no scrolling through hundreds of lines.
- 🤝 **Your accountant will love this.** Hand over one clean, structured Excel — and likely shrink your accounting bill.
- ⏱️ **Hours → minutes.** A year of statements becomes a clear overview in the time it takes to drink a coffee.
- 🧭 **Clarity that drives decisions.** See which clients truly fuel you, where money quietly leaks, and what you actually pay yourself.
- 🏦 **Any bank, any currency, multiple accounts.** It adapts to your data and keeps every balance straight.
- 🔒 **Your data stays yours.** It runs with *your* AI — nothing is uploaded to a finance app or shared with strangers.
- 🔁 **Reusable every year.** Same tool, new statements — a yearly ritual of clarity.
- ✅ **Nothing lost.** Every transaction is reconciled into exactly one category, so the picture is complete.

> This isn't bookkeeping. It's sovereignty. Your numbers were always speaking — now you can finally hear them.

## 📂 What you get

One Excel workbook with **six clear tabs**:

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **1 · All Transactions** | Every movement, chronological — income, expense, balance — totalled per currency |
| **2 · Creditors – Detail** | Every payment to suppliers, by vendor and date |
| **3 · Creditors – Summary** | One line per supplier, with totals |
| **4 · Debtors** | Your real revenue, by client |
| **5 · Owner Account** | Your salary/draws and your own deposits, with the net |
| **6 · Internal Transfers** | Moves between your own accounts (no real money in/out) |

## 🚀 Two ways to use it

### 1 · The Prompt — for everyone
A ready-made text you paste into **any capable AI** — Claude or ChatGPT (with Data Analysis). Upload your statements, paste the prompt, answer a few questions. No setup, works with any bank.

👉 **[`PROMPT.md`](./PROMPT.md)** — copy it and go.

### 2 · The Skill — for Claude Code users
An installable skill for people who use **Claude Code**. More automated: the AI reads your files itself and builds the Excel directly.

👉 See **[`skill/`](./skill/)** and **[`skill/INSTALL.md`](./skill/INSTALL.md)**.

---

## ⚡ Quick start (the prompt)

1. Open **Claude** (file/analysis feature) or **ChatGPT** (*Data Analysis / Code Interpreter*, in the paid plans).
2. Upload your bank statements for the year — **CSV or Excel** work best; PDF is fine too.
3. Paste the prompt from [`PROMPT.md`](./PROMPT.md) and answer the few questions it asks.
4. Download your finished `.xlsx`.

A plain chat without file upload can only return tables as text — not a finished Excel file.
